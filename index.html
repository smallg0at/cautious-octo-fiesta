A character in novel “Private Peaceful”.

He is one of the older brother of the protagonist, The oldest child in the Peaceful Family.

He is kind, optimistic, selfless. Big Joe is a happiness source for the family. He is always happy, and forgive others frequently.

History

Before the storyline

Big Joe was born in around 1890s.

A few days after he was born, he encountered some brain damage somehow, which lead to mental disability.

Due to his mental disability, he was unable to attend schools.

The Change of Attitude

At first his two brothers didn’t care about him. They even force him to eat rabbit droppings.

But things changed when his mother was angry with his brothers. They felt ashamed and began to show Big Joe respect.

As a result, everyone in his family except Grandma Wolf treated him well.

They hated the one who didn’t like Big Joe and were kind to the people who like Big Joe.

Brothers’ Relationship

Big Joe did not mind that the family was revolving around him, and he often hang out with his brothers.

One of them that the author mentioned was a fishing trip with Big Joe, when his greeting to the Colonel seems to offend him.

Big Joe caused Tommo’s first fight because of others’ disrespect to Big Joe, then causing Molly to stick with the Peaceful Family.
